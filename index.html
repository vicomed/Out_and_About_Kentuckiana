Out and About Kentuckiana, Inc. (501c3)

We strive to fight the social isolation that can often come with advanced age or a disability. We believe that Kentuckiana has the capacity to be enhanced through the sharing of unique gifts, talents and contributions of all its members. A feeling of kinship with others and opportunities to participate in community life maximizes health of mind, body and spirit. Out and About Kentuckiana, Inc provides the means to participate in community activities through our community participation grants and the Cycling Without Age program.

Cycling Without Age Louisville

In a society with a growing number of elderly people living in care or alone at home, Cycling Without Age Louisville addresses many social and well-being concerns.

The act of two passengers sharing a trishaw, along with the volunteer pilots, creates multi-generational relationships and friendships, which has proven to be one of the most valuable aspects of the program.

Cycling Without Age Louisville promotes social engagement of seniors and of those with disabilities, and is just one way Out and About Kentuckiana Inc. is improving community inclusion and reducing loneliness.
