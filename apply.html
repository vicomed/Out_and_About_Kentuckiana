To refer yourself or another person, please download, print, and fill out our application. Applicants must be at least 21 years old.

Funding for any request is contingent upon availability of funds.

Return completed applications to:
OutandAboutKentuckiana@yahoo.com
or by mail to
P.O. Box 175
Prospect, KY 40059

Examples of requests include but are not limited to:

  • Tickets to local museums and attractions, sporting events, arts and culture events or performances (theatre, concerts).
  • Financial assistance to participate in local recreational pursuits, such as amusement parks, arts and craft experiences, bowling, swimming, skating, ropes courses, etc.
  • Financial assistance for transportation to and from the activity, or for a care companion to join you.
  • Free trishaw rides for individuals unable to propel a bike.

What cannot be requested:

  • Activities or groups that do not provide for community inclusion and integration.
  • Segregated activities.
  • Any activity in which the participant does not have full control.
