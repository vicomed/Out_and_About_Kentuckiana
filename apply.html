Out and About Kentuckiana
Community Participation Grants

We provide grants to deserving adults with disabilities to decrease social isolation.

Often people with disabilities are not well integrated into the community. OAK provides services so that individuals can participate in activities we often take for granted, such as: a concert, ballgame, or a zoo membership!

To refer yourself or another for a Community Participation Grant, please download the application and mail it to:
P.O. Box 175
Prospect, KY 40059
or by email to:
outandaboutkentuckiana@yahoo.com

Community Participation Grants are contingent on funding.
